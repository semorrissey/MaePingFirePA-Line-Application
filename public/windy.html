Windy.com Map Tool


You can use this map to look at the temperature and wind speed in and around Thailand. Use the button in the top right hand corner to change Map Types. Click the play button in the bottom left to play and pause the timeline

If you would like to view more information you can click on the Windy icon that is located at the top of the map. This will take you to the official Windy website where you can access information on air quality, rain and thunder, clouds, waves, humidity, PM2.5, visibility, and ozone.
